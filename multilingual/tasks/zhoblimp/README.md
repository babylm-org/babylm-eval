# ZhoBLIMP (Chinese BLIMP)

### Paper

Title: `ZhoBLIMP: Chinese Benchmark of Linguistic Minimal Pairs`

Homepage: https://huggingface.co/datasets/Junrui1202/zhoblimp

ZhoBLIMP is the Chinese version of BLIMP (Benchmark of Linguistic Minimal Pairs). It tests linguistic phenomena in Chinese through minimal pairs of grammatical and ungrammatical sentences.

### Groups and Tasks
